General Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to provide shipping and payment information, then confirm your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Cash on Delivery, Credit/Debit Cards (via Stripe), and PayPal.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also view your order status in your account dashboard.

Shipping & Returns

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please refer to our full Return Policy page for more details.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery usually takes 2-3 business days within Bangladesh. International shipping times may vary.

Fashion Tips for Summer 2025

July 15, 2025 | By Admin

Stay stylish and comfortable this summer with our top fashion tips and must-have wardrobe essentials.

Summer 2025 brings a refreshing mix of retro styles and futuristic fabrics. The biggest trend this season is sustainable fashion, with many brands offering clothing made from recycled materials and innovative plant-based fabrics. Oversized silhouettes remain popular, but with a twist - think structured shoulders and defined waists to balance the volume.

Color-wise, we're seeing a lot of vibrant citrus tones paired with cool neutrals. Accessories are bold but minimal - one statement piece per outfit. Footwear trends include chunky sandals and breathable sneakers designed for both style and comfort in the summer heat.

Decorate Your Home on a Budget

July 10, 2025 | By Admin

Transform your living space without breaking the bank with these creative and affordable home decor ideas.

Decorating on a budget doesn't mean sacrificing style. Start with a fresh coat of paint - it's the most cost-effective way to transform a room. Consider an accent wall in a bold color or try the latest textured painting techniques. Thrift stores and flea markets are treasure troves for unique furniture pieces that can be refurbished with some sandpaper and paint.

DIY projects can add personal touches - try making your own wall art or decorative pillows. Plants are another budget-friendly way to add life to your space. For lighting, swap out old lampshades or add dimmer switches to create ambiance. Remember, less is often more - a few well-chosen pieces can make a bigger impact than a cluttered space full of inexpensive items.
